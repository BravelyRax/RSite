SERVICES

I work with recurring-revenue businesses where keeping a customer is worth far more than winning a new one.

Retention & Customer Success

A proactive, concierge success model — reaching customers in the first weeks before frustration turns into a cancellation, adjusting before they walk, and extending how long they stay. This is the work that cut early-cycle cancellations by a third and lifted average subscriber lifetime by roughly 30% at a Singapore subscription business.

If customers are leaking out faster than they should, let's talk.

Onboarding & Ops Systems

I build the systems retention runs on: the onboarding sequence, the playbooks and ops manual, and a voice-of-customer loop that pools and triages problems and routes them to the teams who can actually fix them — so you're removing the causes of churn, not just answering tickets about it.

I've built the onboarding document a company's first hire was handed on day one — more than once.

Diagnosis & Small Fixes

I get inside the product to find where the experience breaks — reproducing the issue and tracing the workflow end to end. And using AI tools like Cursor and Claude Code, I can ship the smaller fixes myself rather than filing them and waiting.

I'm not an engineer, and I won't pretend to be one. But I don't just report the problem — I can close the small ones.
